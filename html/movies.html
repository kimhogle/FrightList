Have a look at my top favorite horror movies of all time. Click the coffins below.

Return of the Living Dead

1985

Punk rockers. Zombies. BRAINS! This amazing movie has my favorite zombie ever, TARMAN.

The Phantom of the Opera

1925

This silent film is so visually stunning. The phantom walking down the stairs in the red outfit and skull mask is super epic!

Nosferatu

1922

Yet another classic horror movie from the 1920s! This was one of the first older black and white horror films I watched, and it did not disappoint. It still gives me chills.

Gremlins

1984

This movie is one of everybody's favorites. Everybody's favorite Mogwai movie! It's funny and just all around amazing.

Halloween

1978

The original Halloween has the best theme song ever that just gets me so excited. Michael Myers is just relentless.

Halloween III: Season of the Witch

1982

Completely different from it's predecessor, this movie does NOT involve Michael Myers, but boy, it does not let down. From the silver shamrock song to the action and ending, this movie rules.

A Nightmare On Elm Street 3: Dream Warriors

1987

This is probably my favorite Nightmre on Elm Street movie. Freddy is MENACING.

The Lost Boys

1987

This is one of the coolest vampire movies ever. There's so much action and I've been dying to visit Santa Carla.

Nightbreed

1990

This movie is so wild, with creatures of the night that you've never seen before. Midian Cemetery looks amazing.

Scream

1996

This movie was probably one of my first horror movies, giving it a special place in my heart. Ghostface is wicked.

Haunt

2019

This movie takes place in a haunted house. It takes one of my biggest fears and brings it to life, which gives me chills.

Elvira: Mistress of the Dark

1988

Elvira is my absolute FAVORITE horror icon. She's THE Queen of Halloween, she's hilarious, witty, dark, and a complete bombshell.

Trick 'r Treat

2007

This movie has a few short stories, with the most adorably creepy character names Sam. It's visually stunning, and the stories are all so original. It's the perfect Halloween watch.

V/H/S/2

2013

Yet another horror movie full of short stories, this found-footage film is WILD. From cults to alines and more, it is one wildly awesome ride.

Malignant

2021

This movie has a very unexpected twist in it that had be laughing so hard, but that's what makes me love it. There's a lot of controversy over it being good or bad, but to each their own. I found it original and fun.

Halloween

1978

This movie has one of the most gruesome scenes I've seen in any horror movie, making it one of my favorites. Art the Clown is iconic and brings fear to all those who fear clowns, and those who don't.

The Fly

1986

The Fly has it all. From romance, to science, the amazing Jeff Goldblum really brought this film to life.

As Above, So Below

2014

This is another one of my absolute favorite found-footage films. It takes place in the catacombs of Paris, and gives me serious chills. It is completely wild.

Creep

2014

This movie (which is yet another found-footage film) gives an up-close and personal look at a real psycho. It made me laugh a few times and is so fun to watch.

The Craft

1996

A complete classic, this is another one of my first horror movies, which I think most everyone loves. An amazing 90s witches movie that never gets old.
