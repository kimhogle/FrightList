Have a look at my top favorite horror shows of all time. Click the coffins below.

Dexter

2006-2013

Dexter is sooo good! A serial killer that only kills by his code? Awesome!

American Horror Story

2011-Current

Each season of AHS is different than the others. Coven and Hotel are my two favorite seaons.

Scream Queens

2015-2016

This show was so funny, fun, and original.

The Munsters

1964-1966

I absolutely LOVE The Munsters. Herman is so goofy, Grandpa is hilarious, and Lily is one of my favorite horror icons ever because I relate to her.

Tales from the Crypt

1989-1996

The Crypt Keeper is soooo cool and has witty jokes each episode. Each episode is its each little tale of terror.

What We Do in the Shadows

2019-Current

This show is HILARIOUS. Vampires that have been alive for hundreds of years trying to live in the present day? I'm all in.

Archive 81

2022

Although only lasting one short season on Netflix, this show was full of mystery and mind-twists.

Brand New Cherry Flavor

2021

Yet another awesome show that only lasted one season on Netflix, this show was willldddd and original.

Bates Motel

2013-2017

An intimate prequel to the lovely Norma and Norman Bates from Psycho, this show was amazing.

Chilling Adventures of Sabrina

2018-2020

Sabrina starts as somewhat innocent then really starts to let out her wicked witchy side as a half-witch in this fun, crazy show.

Stranger Things

2016-Current

The major hit Netflix show had me sit and watch almost the whole first season in one day when it came out. An original heavily inspired by the 80s stole my (as well as so many others) heart.

Midnight Mass

2021

A slow burn, this show has quite the vampiric twist on a small island.

Ash vs Evil Dead

2015-2018

Bruce Campbell and his team take on more deadites in this adventurous and gory show.

Twin Peaks

1990-1991

Although more of a mystery and sci-fi, the characters of this show and the mysteries behind the death of one Laura Palmer kept me watching non-stop.

Black Mirror

2016-Current

This show is a scary and dark look into a possible future. Every episode is a different story that isn't too far from what could be in our future.
